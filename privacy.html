DoseTick Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how DoseTick handles user information.

1. Operator

  • Developer name: KENTA OSAWA
  • Contact: keyroid.ai@gmail.com

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the handling of information in connection with DoseTick.

If the user opens an external website, store page, browser, or email app from DoseTick, the policies and behavior of those external services apply.

3. Information Collected

DoseTick does not require account registration or login.

DoseTick does not send user records to a developer server.

3.1 Information Entered by the User

The user may enter the following information in the app:

  • Medication or supplement name
  • Amount
  • Unit

3.2 Information Stored on the Device

DoseTick stores the following information locally on the user's device:

  • Medication or supplement item list
  • Intake history
  • Display language setting

This information is used to display the user's local records in the app.

3.3 Automatically Collected Information

DoseTick does not automatically collect user information.

3.4 Third-Party SDKs and External Services

DoseTick does not use Firebase, Crashlytics, Analytics, AdMob, RevenueCat, Google Play Billing, or other advertising, analytics, tracking, or billing SDKs.

When the user opens the Privacy Policy, the operating system or an external browser may be used. Any communication or access logs outside the app are handled according to the external service, browser, or operating system.

4. Purpose of Use

Locally stored information is used for the following purposes:

  • Displaying the medication or supplement item list
  • Displaying intake history
  • Keeping the display language setting
  • Responding to user inquiries
  • Responding as required by law

5. Storage

  • Storage method: data is stored locally on the user's device.
  • Server transmission: data is not sent to the developer's server.
  • Cloud sync: not provided.
  • App-managed device transfer: not provided.
  • App-managed backup: not provided.
  • App-managed restore: not provided.

6. Third-Party Sharing

DoseTick does not provide user information to third parties except where required by law.

DoseTick does not use third-party SDKs for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

7. Data Management

Information stored on the device is managed within the user's device environment. Data may be lost if the device is lost, damaged, changed, reset, or if the app is deleted or reinstalled.

Operating system migration or backup features, such as iCloud backup or Android backup, may transfer data depending on the user's device, account, and backup settings. DoseTick does not provide or guarantee app-managed transfer, cloud sync, backup, or restore.

Because DoseTick does not send intake records to a developer server, the developer cannot recover records that were not restored after a device change or reinstall.

8. Retention and Deletion

Information stored on the device remains on the device until the user deletes it.

The user can delete the following local data by using the in-app local data deletion feature:

  • Medication or supplement item list
  • Intake history
  • Display language setting

DoseTick does not provide account registration, so there is no server-side account data to delete.

9. User Review, Correction, and Deletion

The user can review, edit, and delete records using the app's local features.

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact keyroid.ai@gmail.com.

10. Use by Minors

Minors should use the app with consent from a parent or guardian where appropriate.

11. Medical Notice

DoseTick is a record-keeping tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, medication instructions, or dosage calculation.

For medical decisions, medication changes, or medication discontinuation, consult a doctor, pharmacist, or other qualified healthcare professional.

12. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be changed when necessary due to changes in law, app functionality, or other circumstances. Important changes may be announced in the app or on the public page.
